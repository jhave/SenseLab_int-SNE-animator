Anarchival Tendencies: Movements of Thought 
Sydney
Dec 5 2014
Led by Immediations Collaborator Ramona Benveniste. Working with techniques invented and practiced as part of the Montreal-based Movements of Thought monthly gatherings (2014-2015), this workshop invited graduate students from NIEA to experiment with the event-potential of material thinking. 26 SenseLab members, 35 participants.
