Led by Immediations Collaborator Najmeh Kalili.  Founded by Dr Najmeh, Khalili Mahani in July 2013, and sponsored by SenseLab, Brain, Self and Society is a knowledge-transfer group that gathers around "popular" 
neuroscience topics, to discuss the philosophical, sociological and political implications of "Neurotalk", and "neuroeconomics". 15-30 participants.
