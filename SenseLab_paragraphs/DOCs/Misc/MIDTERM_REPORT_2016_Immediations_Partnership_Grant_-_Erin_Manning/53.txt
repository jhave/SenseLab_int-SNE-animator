Oka
July 25-27 2016
The question of value is key to the Immediations grant. The Oka Alter-Economies Study Retreat was an opportunity to learn about new peer-to-peer financial platforms that lend themselves to creative use by cooperatives operating according to the concept of the common rather than in the interests of private gain. In collaboration with Akseli Virtanen and Pekko Koskinen of the Robin Hood Cooperative the result was a proposition for an "Adventure Capital" platform for collective event self-organizing and self-funding. This marks the beginning of the digital platform for the anarchive. 28 participants.
