Led by PhD student and Immediations Collaborator Justy Phillips and Immediations Collaborator Csenge Kolosvari. Event proposition: “We owe each other the indeterminate. We owe each other everything.” This event-series explores the ways in which story-telling shares in a crafting of a collective emergence of a certain precarity. How does the anarchive collect this tentativeness? 15-25 participants, depending on the occasion.
