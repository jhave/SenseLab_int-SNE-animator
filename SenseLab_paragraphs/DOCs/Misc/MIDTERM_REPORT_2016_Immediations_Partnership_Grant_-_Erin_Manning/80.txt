Boston
Oct 3-5 2014
Collaboration between SenseLab and Immediations Partner DS4SI (Design Studio for Social Intervention). Interactive and generative gathering to explore how social movements can learn from dance as movement. 16 members of SenseLab, 35 participants.
Improvisation Practices Symposium
Sydney
Nov 21-Dec 7 2014
Collaboration between SenseLab and Immediations Partner Critical Path.  The event brought together dancers, performers and thinkers around the question of improvisation in performance. There were three afternoon sessions in the middle of the two week event run by grant co-applicant Jondi Keane with internationally known expert on performance improvisation Andrew Morrish. 20 members of SenseLab, 40 participants.
