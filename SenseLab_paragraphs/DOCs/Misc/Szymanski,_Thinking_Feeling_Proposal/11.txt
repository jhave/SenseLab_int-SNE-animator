Author Bio: Adam is a PhD student at Concordia University whose current research is focused on the aesthetics of melancholy in contemporary global art cinema cinema. Adam has published on Félix Guattari and the theory of minor cinema in the journal Kinephanos and on Walter Benjamin and fashion photography in Film, Fashion and Consumption. Adam is an active member of the SenseLab, an international collective for research-creation and works as publication co-ordinator of the Senselab-run journal Inflexions managing editor of the new Immediations book series.
