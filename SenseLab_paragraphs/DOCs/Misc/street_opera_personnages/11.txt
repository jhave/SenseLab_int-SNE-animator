The main character is almost mute, the past characters have the floor (singers and actors), the present ones are dancers and musicians.
Il s’agit d’une oeuvre chorale où chaque personnage est une force ré-active déclinée entre les mondes passé et présent. L’héroîne devra déjouer cet environnement social pour trouver une nouvelle forme de parole.
This is a choral work in which each character is a re-active force and varies between the past and present worlds. The heroine will have to undo this social environment in order to find a new voice.
