Le roi est le pilier de l'État. Le père est le pilier de la famille. Le mari est le pilier de la femme. -- Confucius (5e siècle av-JC)  Maxime à déjouer au cours de notre performance! 
The king is the pillar of the State. The father is the pillar of the family. The husband is the pillar of the wife. (Confucius)
This maxim should constantly be undone during our performance to avoid the traps of easy psychologization!
