Topics concerned the philosophical basis for research-creation, the "ethio-aesthetic paradigm" informing the Immediations project,  new forms of knowledge suggested by that paradigm, the question of what new forms of value these involve, and research results related to the project themes "Recomposing Experience" (the relation between the senses, relations between the senses and langauge, etc.) and "Anarchiving" (what constitutes an event, what traces it leaves, and how it may repeat and vary)
