Movements of Thought panel 
Mayra, Leslie, Diego, Ramona, Csenge, Justy
A double-panel was proposed, with K.O.T. and M.O.T. hosting respectively, but with an invitation to weft and warp across the two. This was an invitation to invent techniques for entering both from the middle, and for both/and rather than either or. Rather than choosing to attend one or the other, a way to work in the ‘between’ of both/and, was proposed. More than simply moving from one panel to the other a warp and weft 
was propositions were crafting through materials, language and movement, for weaving
