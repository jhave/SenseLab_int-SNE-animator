MOT 3, April 9, 12:30-2:30pm - this session oriented around sound art practices, with facilitators Doug Van Nort and Navid Navab (Topological Media Lab), and Julianna Keller (Sonic Electric). Activities and discussion explored deep listening, responsive environments using camera tracking, and the sonic affordances of quotidian objects using contact mics and audio processing. The session was attended by 23 participants.
