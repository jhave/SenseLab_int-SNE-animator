A re-evaluation to the approach to the precarity issue is discussed, refocusing on its collective inception and a collaborative process of making outside the 'editorial team'. Creating a issue that proliferates and invites an eternal return is (re)foreground. An investment in establishing another area in senselab research creation for making and material-based practices is emphasized. Movements of Thought and bi-weekly movement workshops have provided a space for movement-based research, Art Fabulation for film, the reading groups for reading/writing, but the one last area to be established is one were different materials and textures are explored in tangent with those  (M.O.T.) and Knots of thought (K.O.T)
