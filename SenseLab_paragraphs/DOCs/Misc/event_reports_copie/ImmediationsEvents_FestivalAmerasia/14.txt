Ciné-Asie has focused on providing a platform where Asian content, media and digital works can emerge, be discovered, shared and collaborated with. Among many, one of the projects, the 4th edition of the AmérAsia Film Festival sets ahead its substantial facets closely in place in collaboration with the Senselab. The Senselab has opened to interlace new community interactions in partnership with an Asian film showcasing local organization, Ciné-Asie, whose mandate focuses on introducing and discovering Asian film, digital and moving images from the East to the West. The Montreal AmérAsia Film Festival (AmérAsia) is one of the largest annual events at Ciné-Asie which partnered with the Senselab in 2014.
