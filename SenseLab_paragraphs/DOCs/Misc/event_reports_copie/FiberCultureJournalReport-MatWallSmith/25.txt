Description of the Activity: Part of a series of issues supported by Immediations grant and in dialogue with its associated activities that develop publishing architectures and experiments based on enacting and exploring concepts of ‘the minor’ in academic discourse and the intersection of that discourse with contemporary information technologies and architectures. This issue co-edited by Andrew Murphie included 9 peer reviewed articles exploring the contemporary shift to a more molecular environment of software production and exchange.
