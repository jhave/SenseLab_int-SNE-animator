In addition to numerous informal conversations, derives and encounters across Boston and with the associated communities, the residency saw five key undertakings: 
A meet-and-greet with the Fairmount Cultural Corridor and Expression Boston artists 
A round-table lunch and presentation regarding ‘The Undercommons’ to foreground streams of thought and perspectives leading up to the July event
A site-specific creative encounter between the artist-researchers experimenting with the intersection of their research-creation practices
A round-table brunch exploring the theories associated with Senselab’s community based interests and DS4SI’s community development and social justice work
A one hour participatory proposition at the Expressing Boston closing exhibition with more than 50 artists and members of the local community
