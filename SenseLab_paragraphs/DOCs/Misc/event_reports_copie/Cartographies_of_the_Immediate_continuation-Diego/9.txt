Description of the Activity: Continuation of the « Cartographies of the Immediate » workshop event. We witnessed the performance work of students that participated in the latter workshop, and have a dialogue with them together with Sense Lab members, about the activities that could be proposed to continue the collaboration between Sense Lab and HZT BA in Choreography. We also attended the conference on Dance Performance De/Synchronize Living in the Plural in which Erin Manning gave a talk.
