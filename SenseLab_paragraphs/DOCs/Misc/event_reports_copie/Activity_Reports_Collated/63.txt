LOCAL HUB ACTIVITES
Zurich – Zurich University of the Arts (ZHdK)
Participants: Amélie Brisson-Darveau, Christoph Brunner, Alicia Velazquez, Verena Ziegler.
Local planning of activities, research meetings, collaborative writing, and preparation of workshops for conferences on artistic research. Development of a research cluster between Immediations and the Institute for Contemporary Art Research (ZHdK), Institute for Design Research (ZHdK), Department of Architecture at Federal Technical University (ETH), and Institute for Experimental Design and Media Cultures at Academy of Art and Design FHNW.
