Activities: 2013–14
The Australian hub has met regularly via video-conferencing as geographical dispersal requires this. Meetings have occurred on: August 8, 2013, September 2, 2013, September 9, 2013, September 23, 2013; December 10, 2013, February 11, 2014, March 4, 2014, March 31, 2014, April 14, 2014.
These meetings have focussed on discussion of theme one of Immediations – “Recomposing Experience”. Discussion is initiated via circulation of conceptual and practice-based texts and examples ahead of time and major areas to emerge so far are: minor art and research practices, affective listening, cross-modal attunement, improvisation and performance, affective environments.
