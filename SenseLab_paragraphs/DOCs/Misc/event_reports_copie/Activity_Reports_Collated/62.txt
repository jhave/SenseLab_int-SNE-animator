GENERAL HUB-WORKSHOP
April 2014 – Zurich
Present: Jamie Allen, Amélie Brisson Darveau, Christoph Brunner, Nicole de Babrandere, Sher Doruff, Gerko Egert, Jonas Fritsch, Victoria Gray,  Thomas Markussen, Stamatia Portanova, Bodil Marie Stavning Thomsen, Annette Svaneklink Jakobsen, Evelyn Wan, Verena Ziegler.
Workshop on developing the research on the urban fabric from different disciplinary backgrounds. Including conceptual development, strategic planning, artistic experimentation. On April 29, 2014 there was a public presentation and discussion at Corner College in Zurich.
