Governance
The governance committee steering 2014-15 activities, planning, postgraduate student coordination, community liaison and finances allocated to Australian institutions and partners comprises: two institutional representatives, A/Prof. Pia Ednie Brown and A/Prof. Anna Munster, one community partner representative, Prof. Lyndal Jones and one postgraduate student representative, Laura Lotti. This committee has had four formal meetings: June 24, 2013, March 8, 2014 and two joint meeting with the Canadian governance committee on Tuesday December 10, 2013, and Tuesday February 4, 2014.
