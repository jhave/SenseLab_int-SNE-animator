Description of the Activity:
In relation to the EU-Stream of topics on the Urban Fabric and textures, we follow a series of reading sessions from philosophy, architecture, urban studies, media studies, cultural studies, and literature. The skype session also serve as organizational coordination of the EU-Hub activities and physical meetings. The focus revolves around key questions of immediation or the immediacy of experience in urban media environments. The Skype session shape the EU-Hub’s theoretical discourse and interlink different activities in the EU and instigate inter-continental relays between hubs, like emissary visits, smaller workshops, or conference panels.
