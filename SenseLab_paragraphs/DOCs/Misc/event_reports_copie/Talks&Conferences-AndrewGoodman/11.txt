Description of the Activity: Topics concerned research-creation and collaborative approaches to research based on techniques developed within the Immediations project (2, 4) ; immediation and physics (1, 3) ; immediatory experience and ethics, particularly in relation to interactive art and expanded notions of collaboration (1, 2, 3, 4), Whitehead’s concept of feeling (1, 4), transduction (1, 2, 3), ecological and process based approaches to art.
