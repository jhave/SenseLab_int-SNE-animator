The Theatre Season by Vincent Roumagnac
Staging and Spectating Seasons On the Brink of Ecological Collapse.
The Theatre Season is a one year experiment (May 2015 – April 2016 ) that is going on in the vicinity of an outdoor stage located in a public park in Helsinki. It consists of twelve ‘pieces’, tautologically named after the month during which they take place: Piece of May, Piece of June, Piece of July,…
