I speak in the past tense because, though Arakawa and Gins had professed that they had decided not to die, they did - Arakawa in 2010, and Gins this past January, and so the event gathers together personalities from an eminent generation of avant-garde artists, poets, architects etc in NY and beyond (people are flying in from Australia and Europe too). Arakawa was a protégé of Marcel Duchamp and the guest list is a who-s who of the (coolest) generation of NY 60s and 70s artists and activists. More to follow!
