Description of the Activity: Part of a series of issues supported by Immediations grant and in dialogue with its associated activities that develop publishing architectures and experiments based on enacting and exploring concepts of ‘the minor’ in academic discourse and the intersection of that discourse with contemporary information technologies and architectures. This issue included 10 peer reviewed papers and an additional 7 field reports from activists working with a range of technologies and struggling with their many affordances and contradictions.
