Description of the Activity: Part of a series of issues supported by Immediations grant and in dialogue with its associated activities that develop publishing architectures and experiments based on enacting and exploring concepts of ‘the minor’ in academic discourse and the intersection of that discourse with contemporary information technologies and architectures. This issues included 6 peer reviewed papers engaged specifically with the concept of ‘the assemblage’ to think through emerging cultures of imaging and image making.
