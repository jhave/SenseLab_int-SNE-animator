12-13 march: This first phase of the event consisted in reading out loud Plankton Dreams, and creating mobile architectures.
14th of March: a day to develop a sense of sensitivity towards materials and how they can be used in propositions for making. This was thought as a “potential for encounter”, and as an opportunity to bring to materials the same kind of care we have for the texts. 
15-16 march: through the format of a book launch, Tito Mukhopadhyay joined us to discuss his new book.
17-18 march: experimentation with the concept of the anarchive at the Colloque-laboratoire de recherche-création L’Imprévu : de l’erreur à l’errance.
