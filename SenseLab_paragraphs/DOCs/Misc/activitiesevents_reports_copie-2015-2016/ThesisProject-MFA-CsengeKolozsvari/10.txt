One week long exhibition of thesis work (completing an MFA in Fine Arts, Concordia University) co-supervised by Erin Manning. Both the artwork and the written thesis component was heavily influenced by the work done at the Senselab, more particularly engages with research-creation process around the questions of: polyrhythmic movement, topographies of elasticity, the interval and the interference in the repetition of the almost-the-same, accumulation and acceleration.
