Presentation by Patricia Pisters (Prof., University of Amsterdam, Cinema and Media Studies department)
Patricia Pisters is professor of film studies and media culture at the department of Media Studies of the University of Amsterdam and director of the Amsterdam School of Cultural Analysis (ASCA). She has published on film-philosophical questions on the nature of perception, the ontology of the image, on politics of contemporary screen culture and the idea of the “brain as screen” in connection to neuroscience.  Publications include The Matrix of Visual Culture (Stanford University Press, 2003), Mind the Screen (ed. with Jaap Kooijman and Wanda Strauven, Amsterdam University Press, 2008) and The Neuro-Image (Stanford University Press, 2012).
