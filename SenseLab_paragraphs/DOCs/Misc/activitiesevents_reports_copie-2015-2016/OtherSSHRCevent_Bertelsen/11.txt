Workshop on affect, with special emphasis on education and new materialism for academics and practitioners working on affect in Australia, especially Education/Curriculum academics. As part of this project we have organised a second, larger, two day workshop for May 12 and 13, 2016. This workshop is titled : Propositions for affect 2 : Affect, difference, fugitive mobilisation.
