Description of the Activity:
The Immediations@MOLS organized by the EU Hub brought together project partners and outisde participant in the joint exploration of landscapes and water through research-creations workshops on sound, smell and water drawings, readings and performative engagements with the Mols landscape through a number of techniques. It extended the engagment with Urban Fabric(s) into experiments with material and political engagements concerning landscape and water and with an interest in developing anarchival techniques of event-ful continuity.
