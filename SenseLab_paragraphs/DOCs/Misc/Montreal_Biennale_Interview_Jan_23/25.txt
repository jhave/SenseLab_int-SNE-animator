Where does this leave political art, queer art? Must we assign to these artistic sensibilities the duty to smash normative conceptions of the future? To proclaim, “no future!” and bathe in all of the exclamation’s radical negativity? Or is their task a speculative rather than de(con)structive one, in that they must speculate on the potential of the present at each juncture that is encountered, lived and created by a desiring-group’s movement?
