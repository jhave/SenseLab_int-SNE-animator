For, we are not properly thinking the speculative if we are not ready yet to feel the impossible, to live the unlivable which forces us to live, which forces us to think, if we are not ready yet to think at the limit: the force of a life modulating all forms of life and their existential, enunciative modalities. Life, after all, is already a praxis, a pragmatism, a doing, it is, in and of itself, performative of the present.
