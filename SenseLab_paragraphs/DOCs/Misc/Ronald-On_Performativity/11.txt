Our theories hold their own obsolescence. One day, they will no longer pay. Experience invites and entices us all to perform, to experiment, artfully, its own performativity and dismantle our own opinions and clichés. So—to rephrase my opening remarks and questions about the energetic potentility of « this » indefinite moment—in what sense can the performative force of life, the « art of life » or the life in art, in seeking intensity, change, mutation be experimented?
