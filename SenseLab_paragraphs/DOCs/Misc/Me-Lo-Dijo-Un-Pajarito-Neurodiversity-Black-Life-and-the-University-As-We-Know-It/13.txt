What interests me are these termite-ridden walls and the questions they ask, urgently, about whether the sites of power/knowledge we build and sustain are really equal to those who inhabit them? 
Spinoza speaks of the institution as a pact, reminding us that what we live in is also what we build, and what we take down. What is the pact the university demands? What bodies does it need to survive? What knowledges?
