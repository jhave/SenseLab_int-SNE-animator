7) Activity Type:  Immediations Anthology draft – trans-hubs
This is a research outcome of the first 2 years of the project. The anthology is at pre-print stage and is being edited by Erin Manning, Anna Munster and Bodil Marie Stavning Thomsen (one editor from each of the project hubs). The purpose of the anthology is to develop and invent the concept of ‘immediation’ across art, media and event. The anthology will be published by Open Humanities Press and will include experiemental research-creation contributions.
