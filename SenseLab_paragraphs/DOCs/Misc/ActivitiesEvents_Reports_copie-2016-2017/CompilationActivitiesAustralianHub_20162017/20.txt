Activity Type: Event-series, 2nd workshop in the Propositions for Affect Series, funded by a Canadian SSHRC Connection grant, “New Materialisms and Educational Research”, directed by Stephanie Springgay. 
The event was co-organised by Stephanie Springgay, Lone Bertelsen, Andrew Murphie, and Kalvero Gulson.
During the period 2015-2016 Lone Bertelsen was a collaborator on the grant and Andrew Murphie was a co-applicant.
