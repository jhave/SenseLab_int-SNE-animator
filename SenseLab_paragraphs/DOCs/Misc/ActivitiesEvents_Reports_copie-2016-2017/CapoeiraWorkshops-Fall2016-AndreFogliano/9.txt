Description of the Activity: The activity consisted in two basic things. 1) A workshop with the basic moviments, songs and histories of the brazilian street marcial art. 2) Present capoeira as the movement of  black decolonization in Brazil. Created by the diasporic black people in the country, capoeira is a sinchretic ritual that remix martial art, music, dance, play, poetry, spirituality, street knoledges, a complex bodying that expresses the struggle  of slaves for freedom.
