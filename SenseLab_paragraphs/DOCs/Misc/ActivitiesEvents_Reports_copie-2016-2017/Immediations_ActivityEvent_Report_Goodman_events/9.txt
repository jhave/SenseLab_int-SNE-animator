Description of the Activity: 
1/ Regular (fortnightly) reading group focusing on Process philosophy. The group read William James’ ‘Radical empiricism’, A.N. Whitehead’s ‘Modes of Thought’, Deleuze & Guattari’s ‘What is Philosophy ?’.
2/ 9-week seminar series focusing on ‘The Undercommons’ and accompanying readings around radical pedagogy, immanent critique, neuro-diversity, movement and sensation, collaborative practice.
