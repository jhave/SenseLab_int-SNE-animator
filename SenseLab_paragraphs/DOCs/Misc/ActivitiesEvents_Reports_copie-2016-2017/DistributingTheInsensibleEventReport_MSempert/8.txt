Description of the Activity: The event commenced on the heels of the Whitehead conference (December 4-6, Claremont, California) where many of the participants (approximately 20) carried with them the seeds of lived abstractions to be germinated in the « Distribution of the Insensibles. » An exploration around an ethics of care carried the propositional force into the event, and hovered expectantly around the edges as the event gathered its own forces. Reading-gatherings took place with Whitehead’s « Process and Reality » as the lure to feeling and rigour ; the event’s crescendo: an intensive four-day interactive immersive swarm of anarchic activities orbiting around techniques for in-forming ‘books’ that capture the SenseLab’s efforts at eternal possibility-making, that is, the anarchive.
