Calling
The initial calling is an appeal to begin with listening as it emerges from and creates difference, not only difference in terms of contrasts between already formed entities, persons or worlds but a more immanent contrast: a listening emerging as a vibrational cacophony, the polyphony of the situation specific to each ecology, each encounter and each event.
