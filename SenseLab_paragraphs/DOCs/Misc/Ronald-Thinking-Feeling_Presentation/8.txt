An affordance relates to the ability, the aptitude of an agent to activate it in a useful manner. In other words, the direct perception of an affordance in the environment and the capacity of the perceiving agent to activate it have to be understood relationally. Relation is the only answer to what a body can do. It is fairly obvious that, the verticality of the wall will not deter the spider from using it, given its machinic, its technological skills (it is equipped for the type of support the extended surface affords). Much could be said about the orchid now blooming a few meters away from my office: living, understanding its material complexity and fragility I have come to the conclusion that the intensive qualities of my physical self are somewhat excessive in relation to what it affords and what it can support.
