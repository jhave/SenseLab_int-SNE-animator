It follows  a turn  to the  action  of the  movie.  “Concretization” is the  general title that  Simondon attaches to it. Before  he explains this  in the  text,  he directs  our attention back to the photographs filling the interstice between the stills from the beginning and  the end  of the motor  ‘film’. The intersecting images  show  that  the petrol    engine,    confronted   with    the    difficulty   to   transform   its   separate components into an overarching totality, develops “defense structures”.
