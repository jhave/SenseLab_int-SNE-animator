According  to   Simondon,  the   decisive   feature  of  this   process  is  a  loss   of “artificiality”. By means  of concretization, the mode  of existence  of the technical object comes  closer to that  of natural objects. It gains  inner  coherence, becomes  a stage  for multiple functional synergies and,  as a whole,  increases its autonomy with  respect to  the  environment.
