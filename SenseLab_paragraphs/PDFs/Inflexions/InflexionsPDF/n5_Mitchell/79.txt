Where  Transgenic Bacteria Release Machine allowed gallery-goers to  coordinate active  bodily  capacities with  a sense  of being  a (biological) medium  for  other forms  of life, Disembodied Cuisine links  this  experience with  a biotechnological approach to animal life that  understands the  latter  in terms  of functions.
