If screen  size is taken  as synecdoche for the cinema,  we can observe fluctuating mutations in our  contemporary world, where miniaturization accompanies the maximalization   of   the    home    cinema,    where  the    interface   with    gaming technologies is rewriting and  reintegrating the  immersive world of the  “virtual window”, to borrow Anne  Friedberg’s term,  as a cliché for characterizing visions of the future.
