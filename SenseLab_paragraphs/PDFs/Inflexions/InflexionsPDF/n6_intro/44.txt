Indeed, engineering-oriented immortalists have  failed  to consider that  there  is no way to  predict what  effect  longevity will  have on the mind. Organisms that person are not just bodied but embodied, that  is, phenomenologically located  as bodies  that think.   Immortality thus  calls  for  as  much re-engineering  of  conceptual  space   as  it does  of bodily  process.
