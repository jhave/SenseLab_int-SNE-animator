In describing the pleasure of critical events such as Dancing the Virtual, in which participants did not present finished research but rather engaged in “platforms of relation”, Derek McCormack characterizes this collaborative labour by quoting Bruno Latour: “The critic is the one who assembles, who provides arenas in which to gather”. Both in her essay here and in her role as founder of the  Sense Lab, one arena in which Inflexions has flocked together, Erin Manning’s work resonates with this critical disposition. In “Creative Propositions for Thought in Motion”, Manning fabricates such an assemblage from the practical examples of Andrei Tarkovsky,
