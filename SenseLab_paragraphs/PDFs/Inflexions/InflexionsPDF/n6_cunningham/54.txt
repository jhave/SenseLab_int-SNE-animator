In the  teaching method developed by  S. N.  Goenka, and  based  on  the  written teachings of Gautama Buddha,  a prelimanary stage  to Vipassana meditation is Anaparna, a way  of developing mental focus  and  awareness by observing only the breath. Focusing on one’s breath is a crucial  step  in developing the ability  to observe and   recognize  any  sensation in  the  body   “as  it  is,”  no  matter how fleeting  or long lasting,  how subtle  or gross.
