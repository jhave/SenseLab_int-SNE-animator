When  attention to bodily  sensation is practiced in day-to-day life, this  present moment awareness, or dropping into  the moment, enhances my vision:  things I am looking  at become  clearer,  details  more  obvious, actuality more  present. For me, this way  of being  is also linked  to physiological changes. The muscles at the top of my neck relax, the occipital  joint becomes  more fluid, my breathing relaxes and   I'm  more   aware  of  it.
