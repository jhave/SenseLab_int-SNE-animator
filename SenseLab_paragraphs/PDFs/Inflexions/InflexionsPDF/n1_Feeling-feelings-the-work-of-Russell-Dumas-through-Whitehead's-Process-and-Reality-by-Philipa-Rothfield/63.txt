There is a choreographic strategy which  hails from the work  of Twyla Tharp, called ‘the Zs’. A movement phrase is broken up into durational segments and each segment is given  a letter in alphabetical order:  A, B, C and  so on. An adjacent phase  of the phrase is also broken up into As, Bs and  so on. Finally, all the As are reassembled as a movement phrase consisting of the order of As, the Bs are reassembled as the order of Bs and  so on.  The A’s are then  sutured onto
