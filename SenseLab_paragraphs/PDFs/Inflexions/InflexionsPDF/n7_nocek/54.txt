Projects such as “Synthetic Aesthetics,” jointly sponsored by the University of Edinburgh and Stanford University, and funded by the National Science Foundation (NSF) and the Engineering and Physical Sciences Research Council (UK), promote such “reciprocal collaboration,” fostering, as they boast on their website, the “designing, understanding and building [of] the living world” (http://www.syntheticaesthetics.org/).
