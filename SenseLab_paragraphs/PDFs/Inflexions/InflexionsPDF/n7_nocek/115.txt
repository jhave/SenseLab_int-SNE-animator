[3] In her fascinating article, “Symbiotic Architecture: Prehending Digitality,” Luciana Parisi charts two major trends in bio-algorithmic architecture: the use of serial algorithms — genetic and evolutionary algorithms— associated with Neo- Darwinian thinking (Dawkins); and parallel or symbiotic algorithms, based on the logic of symbiotic parasitism, closely associated with Lynn Margulis’ theory of endosymbiosis (Parisi 2009).
