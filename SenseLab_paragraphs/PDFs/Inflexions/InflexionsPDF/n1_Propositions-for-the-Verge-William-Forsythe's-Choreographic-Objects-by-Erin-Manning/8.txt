In Forsythe’s choreographic practice, propositions play a central  role. They elicit action in an environment of change in which  choreography is a multiplying ecology governed by the specificity of a co-constituting environment.
‘Choreography’s manifold incarnations are a perfect  ecology of idea-logics; they
