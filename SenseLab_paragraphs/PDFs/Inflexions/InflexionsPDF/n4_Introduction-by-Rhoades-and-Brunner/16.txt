The proposition of transversality as that  which  is not yet actual  enables  us to draw a potential futurity into the actual  experience of a transversal field  in the moment of its expression.  Such a potential as renewed expression happens through any  encounter with  enunciative instances such  as texts, images,  sounds and  sensations but gains  a particular quality once we aesthetically support such events  in their  transversalizing movement.
