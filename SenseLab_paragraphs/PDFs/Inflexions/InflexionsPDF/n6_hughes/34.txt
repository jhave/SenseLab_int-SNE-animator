In  an  Arakawa and  Gins  architectural surround,  though questions are  posed, considerations  mandated   and    actions    invited,  the   prescribed  answers  or
‘outcomes’ normally associated with  such learning experiences are missing. Here
Gins,   and   Arakawa  are  preoccupied  with   setting   up   the  conditions  for  an unknown transformation, for  an  undesignated ‘X’ to emerge.
