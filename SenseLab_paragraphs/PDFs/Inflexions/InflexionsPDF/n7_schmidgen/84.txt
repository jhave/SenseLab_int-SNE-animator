Repeatedly  he  refers   to  light   machines  and   ballistic   devices: glowing mirrors, Greek  fire, canons,  gun  powder, etc. Willis conceives  of the phenomena of sensori-motricity as explosions or detonations of animal spirits  in the muscles, where they  cause  contractions and  subsequent movements. He also sees the anatomical arrangement of nerves  with  respect to the brain  as a system of light beams.
