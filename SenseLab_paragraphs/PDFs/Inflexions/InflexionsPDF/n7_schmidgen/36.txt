However, this arsenal says little about  the significance of the mechanist reflex concept.  It is undeniable that  this concept  (the “reflex 1850,” as Canguilhem calls it) was coupled in multiple ways  to the laboratory and  explained a large  number of phenomena found and  created in the lab. But according to Canguilhem, these facts  do  not  decide  on  the  scientific   value   of  the  concept   in  question.
