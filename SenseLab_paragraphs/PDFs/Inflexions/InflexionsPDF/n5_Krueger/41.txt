This spontaneous action  is not unique to vibrating plates.  Richard Brown’s Electrochemical Glass  of 1998 is an  experiment trapping between glass  plates galvanically  active   metals   within  an   electrolyte.    Eight   years   after   it  was constructed a new  and  dramatic dendritic growth appeared over  the  course  of several  months (Glanville and  Müller  2008: 30).
