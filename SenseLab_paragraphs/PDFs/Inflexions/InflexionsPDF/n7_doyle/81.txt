The ingredients of the kykeon were revealed in the seventh century B.C. in the so called  Homeric Hymn to Demeter (it was  written by an  anonymous poet  and  not  by  Homer) as follows;  water, barley and  blechon  or glechon a fragrant Mediterranean mint,  probably Mentha pulegium or Mentha aquatica. (Valencic 1994)
