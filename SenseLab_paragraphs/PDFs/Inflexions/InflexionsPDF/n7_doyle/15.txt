This analysis is no critique of Trewavas or Maeterlinck. While rhetorical skill and the  “flowers of  rhetoric”  have   long  been  decried as  dangerous supplements (Derrida) and  the allies of trickster Sophists, Trewavas’ (and  Maeterlinck’s) discussion of plant  ontology requires the  skills  of rhetoric as much  as the  more familiar high  tech  methods used  in the study of plant  signal  transduction.
