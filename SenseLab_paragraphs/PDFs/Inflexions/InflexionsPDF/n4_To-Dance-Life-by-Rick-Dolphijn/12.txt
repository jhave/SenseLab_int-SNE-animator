The body  in the box becomes  a cell, a molecule that does not “contain” but that is always already as a whole  created in the dance. This  (per)formance once  again shows  us  that  dance  is not  about the  body  and  how  it expresses itself. Nor  is dance   about   the  sense  (meaning) released by  a body,   as  Colebrook seems  to ascribe  this idea  to Deleuze (2005: 12).
