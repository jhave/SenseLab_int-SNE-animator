The cinematic and  the animatic should not be thought of as contradictory or competing systems of thought, however. They share  distinct but similar  histories and  are  increasingly used  together in  contemporary filmmaking. Just  like  it is possible to understand some  things about  mechanics through thermodynamics, it is also possible to explain ‘cinematic’ techniques through animation. The point is, rather, that  these  two  techniques shouldn’t be too quickly  elided by thinking
