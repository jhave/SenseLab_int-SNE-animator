Julia  Loktev is a filmmaker  based in  New  York City.   Her  first film,  Moment of Impact, won the director’s award at the 1998  Sundance Film Festival. Her installation work  has been featured at both the Tate Modern and PS1 galleries. Day Night  Day Night  (2006)  is  her  second  feature  film,  and has  won numerous awards, including the Youth Prize at Cannes Director’s Fortnight. She is currently working on a third  feature  film. I  had the pleasure  of interviewing  Julia  in New York City in October 2008.
