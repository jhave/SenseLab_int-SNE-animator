Royal Wedding is also the setting for one of Astaire’s most famous numbers, the “dancing on the ceiling sequence” to the song “You mean  the world to me”. Here, Astaire  enters  a room  with  a stolen  photo of his object of affection.  He begins  by dancing and  singing to the photograph, but then places it on a table and  redistributes the force of desire  into the space as a whole.
