From   the  Pythagoras  to  Alfred   North  Whitehead, there   was   no  known abstraction so abstract as to escape  the system  of the universe, and  literary- artistic  modernism was  the conservative reaction against the premonition of emerging, free-standing forms.  Our  abilities  to make  concrete  prophesy are meager, but  we see forms  coming  well  in advance. We live them  before  we know  what  they are. We escape  the system  of the universe.
