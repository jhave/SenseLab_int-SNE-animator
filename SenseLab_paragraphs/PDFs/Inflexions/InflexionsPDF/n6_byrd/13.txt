For the dying ones,  symmetry is the first claim and  necessity of knowledge: for earth  there  is sky; for land,  there  is ocean;  for subject  there  is object; for every  distinguished this, there  is that.  It was a recursive shuffle  from the beginning. There  was  nothing really  there  but  the will to name  a difference that  named its own  naming. No one has ever been much  more  than  a bunch of free-floating and mottled ideas.
