What  we recombine in the events  of life are not abstract orders or elements of an infinite  universe, but elements of finite ‘worlds’ of our own  making. Actions  and activities and  the objectivities and  subjectivities that accompany them  are framed in ‘worlds’ we hold  apart from  the ‘universe.’ We go also beyond the times  and spaces  of the  ‘thinking  subject’  and  those  of the  individual body,  and  become involved  in  spaces   and   projects   which   are  human in  a  more   collective   and
‘public’ sense.  These  spaces  and  projects  incorporate procedures already performed, actions  already taken  and  the  coherencies and  rationalities of these performances and  actions.  We must  by necessity follow  paths already walked,
