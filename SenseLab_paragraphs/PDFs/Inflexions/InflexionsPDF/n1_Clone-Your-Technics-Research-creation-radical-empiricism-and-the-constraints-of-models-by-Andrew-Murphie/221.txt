Why is it necessary for the dog to be ‘always already a dead dog’? It is again because this enables it to function as linking a strongly recognisable and everyday set of embodied sense perceptions (our everyday experience of dogs) to a new and substitute structure - a model - at an abstract level (reflecting upon our own cognition).
