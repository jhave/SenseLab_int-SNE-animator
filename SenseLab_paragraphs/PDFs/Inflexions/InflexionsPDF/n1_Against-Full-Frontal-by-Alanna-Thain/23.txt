Rhythm in chaos is thus  a form of “partial subjectivity”, that feeling of striving that signals  the emerging of an autonomous creativity: “the feeling of verbal  activity  in the active generation of a signifying sound, including motor  elements of articulation, gesture, mime; the feeling of a movement in which  the whole  organism together with the activity  and  the soul of the world are swept along in their concrete  unity”.
