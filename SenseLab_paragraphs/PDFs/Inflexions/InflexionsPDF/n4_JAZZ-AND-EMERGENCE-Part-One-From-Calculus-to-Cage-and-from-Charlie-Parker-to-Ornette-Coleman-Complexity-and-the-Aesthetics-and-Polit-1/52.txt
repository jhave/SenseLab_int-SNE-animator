That  these  tactics  have  their  origins  not only  in the four  master tropes of Aristotle (as we will see in more  detail),  but  in particular geometrical forms  as well as in cognitive schema, makes  the reference to classical rhetoric fruitful, but in a way hitherto unsuspected.  After all, because of the  properties of musical notation since  the  17th  Century as a kind  of calculus (even  temperament;  bars,  staves  and  time  signatures), which  in  effect  freezes time,  the composer can have  operational control  over  the duration as well as the melodic and  harmonic textures of his work  and  the instruments that play it.
