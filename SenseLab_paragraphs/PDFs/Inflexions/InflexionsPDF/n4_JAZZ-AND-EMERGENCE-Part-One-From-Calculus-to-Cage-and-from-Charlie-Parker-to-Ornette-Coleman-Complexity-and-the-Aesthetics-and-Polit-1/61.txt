These aural  “flavors” are determined by the location  of half-steps in the scale.  If one  were  to  listen  to  one  melodic shape beginning on  the  same  note,  such  as "Do," or the first degree or note of the "C" scale, as that  shape becomes  altered by the  shifting location   of  the  half-steps between notes  of  the  mode,   we  would notice  a profound difference in the sound, timbre, and  even  the emotional “feel” of that melody.
