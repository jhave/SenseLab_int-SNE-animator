When  we  visited Yoro  we  were  one  of  perhaps four couples  in  the   large   expanse.  The  overall   effort   was   towards solitary and bemused  contemplation plus   physical exertion. It  was  an  experience akin  to visiting a museum, where works  of art are enshrined for an experience which  is defined  as  different  from   everyday  life,  providing  moments  that   may   help illuminate everyday life but which  do not remain present.
