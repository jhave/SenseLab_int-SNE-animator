Biological  explanations can  be haunted in a similar  way,  where the  unpredictability of life promotes gothic  spaces  haunted by the feeling that something else, something unplanned, can happen. The horror of biological  thought often  comes  through the multiple hauntings of what  could happen as  opposed to  more  abstract horror of  nothingness.
