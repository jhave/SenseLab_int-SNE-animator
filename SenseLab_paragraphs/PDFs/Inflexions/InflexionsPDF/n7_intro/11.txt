We differ  from  Bergson  in two  regards. Firstly,  we encourage a proliferation of explanatory  methods  for   biological   thought,  whereas  Bergson   appealed  to intuition as the  ultimate method. We feel that  it’s  not  so much  that  life resists intellection and  thus  needs  to  be  intuited, but  that  coming  to  terms  with  the complexity   of   living    processes  requires,   perhaps   even    breeds,   multiple theoretical frameworks.
