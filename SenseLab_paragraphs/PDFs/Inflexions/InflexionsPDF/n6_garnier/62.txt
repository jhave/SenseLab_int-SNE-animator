Gregory Ulmer’s  search  for a heuretics, a method or  anti-method promoting a non-logical mode  of reasoning, proposes to  deconstruct linear  paradigms and frontier-based metaphors for research – thus  avoiding what  he calls “associating method  with   colonial   exploration” through  a  revision  of  the  “frontier”  into “chora” (Ulmer   1994: 31).
