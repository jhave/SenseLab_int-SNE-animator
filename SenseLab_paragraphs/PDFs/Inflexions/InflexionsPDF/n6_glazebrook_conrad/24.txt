The openness of the future balances the fixity of the past,  if destiny is to become intentional possibility. History can be understood in different ways,  but understanding the  actuality of past  events  that  are  definitive for contemporary challenges is a crucial  part  of reversing destiny, rather than  merely being  blown by the winds of historical contingency. Consumer culture is at present a radically unsustainable way of living  that  threatens local, regional, and  global  ecosystems.
