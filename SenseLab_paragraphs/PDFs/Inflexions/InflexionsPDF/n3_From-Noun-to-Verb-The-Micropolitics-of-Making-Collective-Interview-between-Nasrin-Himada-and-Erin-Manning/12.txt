As you know, at the SenseLab, we’ve always emphasized, following Guattari, that the modes of encounter for collaborative micropolitical exploration are not based on a personalized understanding of subjectivity. We are not working out of a pre­established notion of friendship, for instance. It is the project, the event, the technique that moves us to work  together. Without the project, there is no group subjectivity, and therefore no collective process.
