Some  architects  are  designing  buildings  with   the  objective   of  their disappearance. Take for example Jochen Gerz’s memorial for the holocaust in Hamburg. Rather  than  the  typical  slate  with  people’s  names, he  built  a  huge phallic  monolith, covered with  a dark  surface:  a big prism.
