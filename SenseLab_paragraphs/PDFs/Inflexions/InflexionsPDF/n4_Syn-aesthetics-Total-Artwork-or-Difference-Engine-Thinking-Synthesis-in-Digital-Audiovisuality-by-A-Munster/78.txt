3  The Cimatics festival  has  been  held  for 7 years  and  is supported by  Cimatics Platform,  an   international  organization  based    in   Brussels   supporting  and exhibiting audiovisual art.  It started as a VJing Festival  in 2003 but has expanded to include an  array  of auidovisual sculptural and  kinetic  events,  masterclasses and  performances.   Its  website can  be  accessed   at:  http://www.cimatics.com.
