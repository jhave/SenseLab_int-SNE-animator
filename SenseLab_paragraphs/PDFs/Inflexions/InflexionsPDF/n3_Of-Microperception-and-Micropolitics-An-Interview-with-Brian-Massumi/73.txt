There’s a certain  incompleteness  to any micropolitical  event, like  the events  I was  talking about.  A  lot  of  things that  you   feel  were on the verge of  taking shape didn’t quite happen. Potentials  that you could  just  glimpse  didn’t come into focus. The goal is not to overcome the incompleteness. It’s to make it compelling.  Compelling  enough that you  are moved to do it again, differently, bringing  out another set  of  potentials,  some more  formed  and focused,  others that were  clearly  expressed  before  now backgrounded.  That  creates  a small, moveable   environment   of   potential.   The   goal   is   to  live   in   that  moveable
