Let’s give an example of a seasonal worker: the artist.  It is artists, for example, that use unemployment insurance. Instead of using the money to soften the risk of being out of work, they use it to finance their  artistic work. Thus, this is a subversion of the meaning of unemployment insurance. At the micropolitical level, everyone does this. It’s a subversion that undermines the categories work-unemployment, juridical categories in the workplace, in the realm of social security. These categories are shifted because unemployment cannot be translated into inactivity (that can only be finalized/activated through the search for employment), but as a moment of artistic activity.
