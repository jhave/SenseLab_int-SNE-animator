We have to limit. I have to limit my freedom, I have to limit my potential for action, I have to limit my capacity to act because effectively you  are my enemy. You are my enemy, not my warring enemy, but a potential adversary. If we are moved by self-interest, the only way to gather and coordinate these egotistical interests is to introduce principles of security. And this is what happens. What Marx says is that we substitute confidence for mechanisms of security that are put into place even at the financial level.
