A  set  of  impossibilities began  to  open  up,  however paradoxically, towards a common  happening,  towards  running  the   risk   of  concluding  that   nothing happened. Nothing, that  there  is no  piece,  but  that  in  that  absence  of a piece something from a common order was able to happen. “A community for the art of not making work,” said Inforsato, resisting prescriptive dynamics—to sustain suspension, the drift, to leave the field open  instead of betting on edifications.
