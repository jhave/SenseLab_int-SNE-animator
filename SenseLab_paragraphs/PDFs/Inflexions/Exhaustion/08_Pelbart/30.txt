A  publishing house  I founded with  other  partners was called  n-1  publications, taking   its  inspiration from  this  phrase in  A  Thousand Plateaus: “In truth, it is not enough to say, ‘Long live the multiple,’ difficult  as it is to raise that cry… The multiple must  be made…  always n-1… Subtract the unique from the multiplicity to be constituted; write  at n-1 dimensions” (1987: 6). Not by chance, the editorial and theatrical line got crossed.
