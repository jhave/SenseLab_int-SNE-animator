In this  process,  individuals are pushed towards the edge  of a sustainable existential field,  at  once  facing  a  plethora of  choices  and  incapable of making a choice or cut that  would deviate from the permanent exhaustive capture. As François  Zourabichvili succinctly said  in Spinozean manner, “everything is possible, yet nothing is possible” (1998: 354).
