Yubraj Aryal: For Deleuze, a crucial political question is: how can we free ourselves from the condition under which we tend to ‘fight for our servitude as stubbornly as though it were our salvation’? Can the performative quality of affective political resistance help prevent us from investing in a social system that constantly represses us, thwarts our interests, and introduces lack into our lives? Can a faith in ‘affect’ help ensure our freedom within or from such powers of domination and control?
