Jonas Fritsch is Assistant Professor in the Department of Aesthetics and Communication, Aarhus University, Denmark. His research centres on a creative thinking of interaction design and affect theory through  practical design experiments carried out at the Centre for Advanced Visualization and Interaction (CAVI) and the Centre for Participatory IT (PIT) at Aarhus. He is a participant in the SenseLab and a researcher on the SenseLab’s ‘Immediations: Media, Art, Event’ international partnership project, and serves on the editorial boards of Inflexions: A Journal for Research-Creation and Conjunctions: Transdisciplinary Journal of Cultural Participation.
