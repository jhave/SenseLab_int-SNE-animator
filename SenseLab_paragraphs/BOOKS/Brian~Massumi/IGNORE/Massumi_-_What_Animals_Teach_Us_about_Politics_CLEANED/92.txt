As we saw in the analysis of supernormal stimuli, instinct thinks gesturally in qualitative blocks. Its gestures effect and envelop “a complete recasting of the whole [ensemble]” involving a “correlative change of all the old elements” (Bergson 1998, 169). It bears on blocks of relation, ensembles of integrally linked experiential qualities. Bearing on qualities, it “distinguish[es] properties” rather than perceiving objects (1998, 189).
