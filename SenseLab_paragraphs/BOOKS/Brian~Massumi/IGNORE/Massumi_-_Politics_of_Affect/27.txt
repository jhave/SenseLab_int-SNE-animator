Jonas Fritsch is Assistant Professor in the Department of Aesthetics and Communication, Aarhus University, Denmark. His research centres on a creative thinking of interaction design and affect theory through  prac- tical design experiments carried out at the Centre for Advanced Visualization and Interaction (CAVI) and the Centre for Participatory IT (PIT) at Aarhus. He is a participant in the SenseLab and a researcher on the SenseLab’s ‘Immediations: Media, Art, Event’ interna- tional partnership project, and serves on the editorial boards of Inflexions: A Journal for Research-Creation and Conjunctions: Transdisciplinary Journal of Cultural Participation.
