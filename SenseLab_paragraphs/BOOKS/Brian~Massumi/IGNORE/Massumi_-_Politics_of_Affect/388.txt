Brian Massumi: This question is of great interest to me. I approach it from the point of view of process philoso- phy, which is centrally concerned with creativity, with where it is found in the world and how it expresses itself. The short answer to that famous question of Spinoza’s, ‘Do we know what a body can do?’ is simply: ‘No!’ There are powers of improvisation, powers of invention in a body that we have only begun to plumb.
