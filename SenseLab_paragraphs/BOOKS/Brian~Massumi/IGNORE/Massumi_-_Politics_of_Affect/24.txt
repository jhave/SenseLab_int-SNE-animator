Arno Boehler is Associate Professor in the Department of Philosophy at the University  of  Vienna,  Austria. He is the founder of the philosophy performance festival ‘Philosophy On Stage’. He leads the research project ‘Artist-Philosophers: Philosophy AS Arts-Based- Research’, University of Applied Arts Vienna, spon- sored by the Austrian Science Fund. He has held fellowships at the Universities of Bangalore, Heidelberg, New York, and Princeton. Further information: http:// homepage.univie.ac.at/arno.boehler
