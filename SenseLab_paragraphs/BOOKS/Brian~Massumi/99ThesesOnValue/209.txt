Scholium c. This is not all emotion is. As always, there is an excess-over any capture: a surplus of affect that is fed forward as surplus-value of life, moving the life-process forward. Emotion is shot through with affect. It carries intensity. Its narrative development is motored by affective intensities, whose expression narrative channels into its own procession. The unitization of affect as emotion is a narrative coding of it. Affect as such is neither unitizable nor codable. It is more-than-narrative. T68 Being more-than-narrative, affect is extra-personal.
