“Like the ovum of the universe segmented” (Peirce 1992b, 262).
A perceptible difference has emerged from vague potential. The continuity of the virtual whole of be-ability has fed forward onto the plane of actual being-different. As been, the whole presents itself twice. Once: in the concrete surface continuity of black and of white. Again: in the pure abstractness of the invisible line separating and connecting the surfaces.
