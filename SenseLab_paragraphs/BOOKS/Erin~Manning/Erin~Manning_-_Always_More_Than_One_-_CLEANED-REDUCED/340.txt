As the participant moved through the “forest” and began to play with the fabric, or as the fabric magnetically caught the participants’ belt or metallic buttons and attached itself, connecting them to its structure, the work began to unfold into its generative architecting. For the magnetic surround from whence the collection was hung was itself an architecture, but not one that was meant to hold in place: the suppleness of the architecture and its magnetic attractors were conceived to facilitate the architecting of a relational environment.
