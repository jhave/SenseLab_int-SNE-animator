The final scene leading up to the documentary footage of the Sabra and Shatila massacre takes thirty minutes to unfold, beginning with the long, slow, close-up of Bashir’s bullet hole–infested face which frames the dancing soldier’s incessant one-two-three. For thirty minutes a back and forth in time from a lawn in Holland to a couch in Israel. And then, finally, we arrive at the scene of the Sabra and Shatila massacre.
