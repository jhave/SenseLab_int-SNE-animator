“At the beginning,” says graphics research specialist Matthew Lewis, William Forsythe’s One Flat Thing, reproduced was “all a bit, ‘what is this about?’ ” Unfazed by Lewis’s confusion, William Forsythe suggested that Lewis think of “what else” the dance could look like. This “what else” is the project of Synchronous Objects—a web-based platform for the visualization of the choreographic as it expresses itself through Forsythe’s staged performance One Flat Thing, reproduced (see figure 14).
