Choreographing the political begins here, in the midst of shapeshifting speciations. Allied to activist philosophy, allied to the kind of study that happens in the undercommons, a choreographing of the political sees minor gestures everywhere at work, and it seizes them. Choreographing the political is a call not only for the collective crafting of minor gestures, but for the attunement, in perception, to how minor gestures do their work.
