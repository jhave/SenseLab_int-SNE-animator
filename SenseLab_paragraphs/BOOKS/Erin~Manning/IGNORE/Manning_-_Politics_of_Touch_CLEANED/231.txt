This is the metamorphosis of the body of which José Gil speaks, a “condition of the activity of code translation: each exfoliation is the metamorpho- sis of all an other forms in a spatial one” (1998, 38). One is the space of relation: there is no uniﬁed body. There are skins, receptive surfaces, gestural movements, desires toward an other. The body is active potential, not tautology.
