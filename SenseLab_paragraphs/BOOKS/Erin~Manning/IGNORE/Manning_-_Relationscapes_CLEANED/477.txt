Arnkerrthe, Mina Mina, and Alhalkere are prehensions of Dreamings. To prehend the Dreaming is to move with it, to compose with it. To prehend the Dreaming involves more than narrating an instance of it. It calls forth the activity of the land’s eventfulness and pulls this eventfulness into the present-passing such that a new actual occasion—a becoming-world—emerges.
