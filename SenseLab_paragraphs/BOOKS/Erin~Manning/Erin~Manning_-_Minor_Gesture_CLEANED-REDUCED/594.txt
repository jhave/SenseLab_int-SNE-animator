Study does not evaluate from outside its process. It values in the doing. Its plan involves creating conditions for an encounter that has not prechoreographed what it can do. “But if you listen to them they will tell you: we will not handle credit, and we cannot handle debt, debt flows through us, and there’s no time to tell you everything, so much bad debt, so much to forget and remember again. But if we listen to them they will say: come let’s plan something together. And that’s what we’re going to do” (2013: 68).
