To feel in the moving, to activate the more-than that coincides both with object likeness and relational fielding, is to experience the nonlinearity of time where nothing is yet, but everything acts.4 Here, there is no succession in the metric sense. To act is to activate as much as to actualize, to make felt the schism between the virtual folds of duration and the actual openings of the now as quality of passage. On its way.
