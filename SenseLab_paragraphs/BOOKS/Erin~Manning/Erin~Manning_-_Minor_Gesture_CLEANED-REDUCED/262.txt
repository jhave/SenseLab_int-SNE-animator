World-constituting never means world-constituted. To craft a procedure that is world-constituting, the fine-tuning must occur in the event—it must be immanent to the event’s coming-into-itself. Fashion that follows habit fully formed is not doing this. It is created according to an externally imposed normative framework. Kawakubo’s practice departs from this approach: she is very much engaged in the constitutive tendencies that open habit to its more-than.
