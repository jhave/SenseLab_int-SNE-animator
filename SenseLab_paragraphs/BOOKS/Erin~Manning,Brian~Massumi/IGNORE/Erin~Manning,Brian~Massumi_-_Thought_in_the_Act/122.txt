All of a sudden, a lingering. And so we are reminded that the suspen- sion is everywhere speciously present, speculatively active at different velocities, notionally in different registers, relationally across every modal- ity of experience. A lingering, a suspension of isolatable movement form, a suspension of language, as designation or narration, and also a suspen- sion, within movement-moving, of its human characters, tuning to their elemental qualities, gesturing to dance’s capacity to hold dissonant times together in movement.
