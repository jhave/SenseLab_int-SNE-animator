The  problem of performatively activating links rather than relying on reportage was  exacerbated by  the distributed nature of  the event. Techniques of relation would have to be invented to connect molecules across cities and countries and even continents. Two  approaches used in previous  events were  adapted to this purpose. The  model of  hospitality was hybridized to become one of diplomacy.32  This  became the technique of the “emissary.”
