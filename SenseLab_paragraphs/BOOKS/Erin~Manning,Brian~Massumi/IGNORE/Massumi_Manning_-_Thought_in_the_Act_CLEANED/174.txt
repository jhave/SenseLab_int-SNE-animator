Movement’s folding in on itself is not something the body does. It is what bodying is.  Movement embodies only itself. Movement’s making is corporeogenic: becoming-body.
Habit’s adaptive action functionally unfolds. Dance movement inventively infolds. New  moves come from movement’s contracting back into its  centerpoint, to re-splay as never before, throwing new curves for  the seeing.
