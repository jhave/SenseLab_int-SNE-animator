Face becoming imperceptible: no name. No title—yet. “I” am all  the more not-there-yet. All the more no-more, and yet:  to come. By No Title Yet No. 3—Eurydice, the interplay of white with itself predominates. This  concentrates the movement without reducing its  intensive spread. The  whole canvas vibrates white. The  movement becomes pulsing. The pulsing takes into itself the painting’s distances, enveloping them in its own movement in place, in an all-over vibratory immediacy. Distance in immediate proximity.
