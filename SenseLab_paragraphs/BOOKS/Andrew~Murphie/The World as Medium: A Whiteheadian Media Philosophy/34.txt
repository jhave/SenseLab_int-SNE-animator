How then is communication to attain some kind of clarity? It does so in terms of the immanent specificity of the way that prehensions are gathered together in a unique actual occasion. As Halewood puts it: “all information … can only be fully understood if the manner in which it presents itself is taken into account. In this sense, the qualitative aspect of all information is an integral aspect of all information so that the world becomes ‘objectively qualitative’ ” (2005a: 79).
