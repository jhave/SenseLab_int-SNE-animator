The world is media, in a manner of sensation and contracting … it is possible to continue from [Deleuze and Guattari’s] philosophy of cosmic vibrations towards directions of a natural philosophy of media where the term starts to encompass the recording of time in rocks, the capacities of transmission in plants and animals, the weird sensations for example in insects that perceive not only through eyes and ears, but through chemicals as well. (2008: 20)
