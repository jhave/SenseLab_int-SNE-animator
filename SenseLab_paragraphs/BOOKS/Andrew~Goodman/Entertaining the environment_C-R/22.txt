The individual is a cut in the flow of unfolding, ‘ a singularity in the plane of individuation’, this individuation an always-in-process, a ‘more than’ reaching towards the next (a body-ing, a cutting, again, always from the middle of things), the body a ‘plural one-ness’44. The becoming-scissors of the hand, the becoming paper of the scissors, or the becoming-cutting of all the components that is their shared potential, their transduction back into forces that transforms not only the paper but the subject.
