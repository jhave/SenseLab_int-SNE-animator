Such transduction integrates, as Simondon states, disparate realities into a system of relation38, which is a relation not only of the actual, but also the virtual or potentiality.
Irreducible multiplicities: the sound of the ocean, wind, fog, flocking birds. The earth’s multiplicities, ‘nebulous set(s)...whose exact deﬁnition escapes us, and who’s local movements are beyond observation’39, that we are thrust into, or rather, born out of (already always re-phasing): growing like grass, Deleuze says, in the middle, always from the middle of things40.
