This interfac(ing) was performed, not, one can say, ever entirely by either the biological nor the technical systems making up the machine, but by the machinic action producing also the potential ruptures and the uncertainty of an evolving dynamic virtual that was its fertility. Here the further potential of interfacing remained present even as it was enacted. It perhaps remained as a ‘lure’ towards feeling, as a pull towards the future, a pre-relational tendency towards affectual relation.
