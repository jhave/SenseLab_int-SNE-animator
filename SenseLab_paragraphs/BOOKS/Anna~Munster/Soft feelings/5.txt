At other times, bodies – yours, mine, the air – become Flume ‘s sensors and transducers, tuning the soft sculpture’s rhythms, fielding the movement of soft feelings around the space, enabling the tonality of light to adjust between bright whiteness and hushed twilight, attenuating the scatters of the auditory topology.
