I want to conclude not with the animal but with the technical, with a becoming self(ie) of the technical as this begins to push humans away from both occupying centrality in processes of subjectivation and stretches the self as a phenomenon generated across participatory entities. In a series of images titled, Drone selfies, the collective IOCOSE speculate about what drones might do if they were not deployed in times of war but instead in times of peace.
